With the success of coins such as $THE Protocol, $TYRANT, and $DIE, we need a tool that provides us with real-time updates in order to capture these moves. After a successful token is created, a lot of short-term plays follow these trends, and for a degen investor, these plays are an opportunity for them to make their next 100x gain.


What We Offer

Let us present you with technology that increases your chances of success. Meta Tools is constantly scanning the Twitter activity of a carefully selected number of 'Influencer' Twitter feeds for Keywords or Keystrings/Phrases. It then cross-references these with the Live Pairs on the ETH blockchain, finding any keyword matches and delivering them to our users. This means that if a Twitter Influencer tweets a specific word that Meta Tools determines is being launched, it will deliver this to our users in near real-time. This will enable investors to invest in what could be the beginning of a highly profitable trend.
